Recipe: Rice Pudding

Rice Pudding with Cherrys. Picture by @rasmusgs at unsplash

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice pudding rice
  • 4-6 cups of milk
  • sugar
  • Flavor e.g.: cherrys, nutmeg, vanilla or raisins

Steps

  1. Put rice and milk in pot
  2. Add sugar and a little salt
  3. Never stop stirring until milk has turned into pudding
  4. add rest of flavouring
