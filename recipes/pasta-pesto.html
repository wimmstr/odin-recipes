Recipe: Pasta with Pesto

This is a very easy yet tasty dish. It only takes like 10 minutes to make and is very hard to mess up. On this picture you can see Farfalle pasta. Picture by @eaterscollective on unsplash.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice, I prefer Farfalle
  • Pesto of choice. You can make your own, but thats for another recipe.
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Parmesano

Steps

  1. Boil about 500 ml of water
  2. Put salt in it
  3. Put pasta in it
  4. Boil for about 10 min, see packaging for details
  5. Pasta on plate
  6. Add a spoon of pesto
  7. Cut 5-7 cherry tomatoes in half and add them on the plate
  8. Grate some parmesano over your dish
  9. Enjoy
